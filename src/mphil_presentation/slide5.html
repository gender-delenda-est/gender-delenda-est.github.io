Methods

Virtual Ethnography

K-Pop fandom is an online/virtual community; while it does manifest offline (concerts, fan cafes etc), this is not where most of the cultural practice of the fandom takes place. It is therefore necessary that my ethnographic practice meet the community here.

This does not mean not using conventional methods--I am using semi-structured interviews for my qualitative work--but necessitates adjusting these.

In particular I recommened reading Boellstorff et al.'s handbook on virtual ethnography.
